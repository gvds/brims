## When to Escalate

Escalate to a project administrator or system administrator when:

- The issue appears to be permission-related
- Data appears inconsistent across linked records
- A shipment or storage discrepancy cannot be reconciled
- Integration settings may be incorrect

## Information to Include When Reporting an Issue

- Project name or identifier
- Relevant subject ID or barcode
- Date and time of the issue
- A short description of what you expected and what happened instead
