# Glossary

## Purpose

Use this glossary to check the key terms used across BRIMS documentation.

## How to Use This Page

Refer to this glossary when a BRIMS term has a specific operational meaning or when similar terms could be confused.

## Terms

### Arm

A study arm is a participant grouping within a project, such as a control group or intervention group.

### Assay

An assay is a laboratory analysis or related record linked to a study and its specimens.

### Event

An event is a scheduled or completed participant activity, such as a visit, follow-up step, or collection point.

### Manifest

A manifest is the shipment or transfer record that groups specimens together for movement between locations.

### Participant

A participant is a person enrolled in a project.

### Project

A project is the top-level unit used to organise research work in BRIMS.

### Site

A site is a physical or organisational location within a project.

### Specimen

A specimen is a biological sample that is logged, tracked, stored, shipped, or used in BRIMS.

### Storage Designation

Storage designation is the project-level identifier used for storage-related operations.

### Study

A study is a research investigation within a project.

### Subject ID

A subject ID is the unique participant identifier generated by BRIMS from the project prefix and digit settings.

### Status

Status is the workflow state used to show the current position of a participant, specimen, event, manifest, or other record.
